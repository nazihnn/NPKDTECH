NPKDTECH Mobile Application

A platform extension for the NPKDTECH robot system designed for soil sampling and analyzing fertilizer levels by detecting nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium in real-time.

App Overview

The NPKDTECH mobile application interfaces with our soil analysis robot to provide farmers and agricultural professionals with real-time soil data. Monitor nutrient levels, control the sampling robot, and make data-driven decisions to optimize crop yields and reduce fertilizer costs.

Real-time Soil Analysis:

View nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium levels in your soil samples as they're collected.

Robot Control System:

Remotely control the NPKDTECH robot to collect samples from specific field locations.

GPS Field Mapping:

Track sample locations with GPS coordinates to create detailed field fertility maps.

Helping The Enviroment:

Tracking NPK data can tackle the issue with overuse of fertelizer which pollutes water sources.

The NPKDTECH Robot

The NPKDTECH robot is designed specifically for precision agriculture, equipped with advanced sensors to detect nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium levels in soil. It autonomously collects samples across your fields and transmits data in real-time to the mobile application.

Key features of the robot include:

  • Automated soil sampling mechanism
  • NPK nutrient detection sensors
  • GPS and UWB positioning for precise navigation
  • Wireless connectivity to the mobile application
  • Weather-resistant design for field operations
  • Rechargeable battery system
